FAQ's
  • Why is the club called ICT & Engineering Club?

    The club is called the ICT & Engineering Club to include both information and communication technology and engineering, allowing us to explore a wider range of skills and foster collaboration among members.

  • When are the general meetings conducted?

    General meetings are held every Tuesday during break in the ICT lab.

  • Who are the members?

    Members include students from all grades interested in technology and engineering. Anyone passionate about learning and innovation is welcome!

  • How to become a member?

    To become a member, fill out and submit the application form. After that, you’ll attend an interview. Bring along 10 dalasi during the interview

  • When will the classes commence?

    Classes will start every Friday morning, from 9:00 t0 11:00 am and a timetable will be provided
