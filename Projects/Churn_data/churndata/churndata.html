SQL Analysis

Complex queries for aggregation and calculations

SELECT category, COUNT(*) as total_records, AVG(value) as avg_value, SUM(value) as total_value FROM cleaned_data WHERE date >= '2024-01-01' GROUP BY category HAVING COUNT(*) > 10 ORDER BY total_value DESC;
